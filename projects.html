In Depth
Taught them the basics of psychical security and what the best ways to secure a psychical building is. We looked at the I LOVE YOU VIRUS and determined what coding practices were used. I taught them about Ubuntu and the tor browser and how it connects to the outside world. I also taught them how to do basic fixing of your windows system with Linux. I also talked about the China chopper web shell and how to prevent viruses from entering your systems. Taught them about stack over flow and how being a CS major also taught them about some of the rules that come with being a CS major. Helped them figure out the professors and classes they are interested in and helped them figure out what they want to be when they get out of the CS major. I also taught them that most of the students can and will help you if you ask them.
